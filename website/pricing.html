Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an audit log?

Any event submitted via the API — agent actions, prompts, responses, guardrail checks, trace events. Batch submissions count each entry individually.

Can I self-host?

Yes. AgentAudit is fully open-source and self-hostable with Docker Compose or bare-metal. See the self-hosting guide for details.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is the trial — no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro or Business when you need more capacity.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

We’ll notify you at 80% and 100% of your monthly limit. You can upgrade instantly or switch to Business for unlimited capacity.
